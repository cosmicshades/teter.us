About

David Teter

If not me, then when? If not me, then who?

David Teter a full time student at Purdue University studying Computer and Information Technology with a minor in Organizational Leadership and Supervision.

In his spare time, he is the Senator for Purdue Polytechnic Institute for Purdue Student Government and Director for the Purdue Computing Career Fair. Through both of these activities, his goal remains to represent the student voice and provide more opportunities to encourage Purdue student success.

David is a strong supporter and participant of Purdue Bands and Orchestras, playing french horn in a variety of different ensembles. He enjoys service through membership in Tau Beta Sigma and the Band Technology Office. In the Technology office, he lends his web development services to the department. He worked with Nick Gould to bring the Purdue 2015 theme to WordPress for use for the University through development of the Purdue Bands and Orchestra’s website.

David serves as lead collaborator and curator at CosmicShades, a distributed WordPress development studio with John Sylvain.
